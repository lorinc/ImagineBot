# Imagine Montessori School Valencia

Imagine Montessori School Valencia is a private, co-educational, and secular educational institution located in Valencia, Spain. It caters to students from Early Years through Secondary (ages 2-18), integrating two distinct yet complementary educational philosophies: the [[Montessori curriculum]] and the [[British National Curriculum of England and Wales]].

## Educational Philosophy and Curriculum

The school's core approach, as detailed in the [[summaries/family_manual]], is to provide a modern education that meets the objectives of the British curriculum while adhering to the methodology and philosophy of Maria Montessori. This unique combination aims to foster student independence, encourage a positive attitude towards learning, and support holistic development—psychological, physical, and emotional.

Key characteristics of its curriculum include:

*   **Dual Accreditation**: Recognized and authorized by both the UK Department for Education (DfES) and the Spanish Ministry of Education.
*   **Creative and Hands-On Learning**: Emphasizes a practical, sensory, and experience-based approach to learning, with classrooms adapted for student accessibility and autonomy.
*   **Prepared Environment**: Classrooms are carefully designed to promote innate curiosity, autonomy, creativity, and a sense of belonging.
*   **Holistic Development**: Focuses on self-confidence, emotional, personal, and social development, alongside academic achievement.
*   **Mixed-Age Classrooms**: Students of different ages interact, promoting cooperation and reducing competition.
*   **Language of Instruction**: English is the primary language of instruction, with a commitment to supporting students' English language development.

## Key Educational Stages and Programs

The school structure encompasses:

*   **[[Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)]]**: Focuses on core areas like Personal, Social, and Emotional Development, Physical Development, and Communication and Language, eventually incorporating Literacy, Mathematics, Understanding the World, and Expressive Arts and Design through Montessori's sensory, cultural, practical life, language, and mathematics areas.
*   **Primary (6-12)**: Integrates National Curriculum standards with five "[[Great Lessons (Montessori)]]" (History of the Universe, Life on Earth, Humanity, Mathematics, and Language) that serve as a framework for in-depth exploration of subjects. It also includes [[STEM education]] enhancement through a "Creative Lab."
*   **Secondary (Y7 to Y13)**: Covers Key Stages 3, 4 (including iGCSE), and 5 (A-Levels), aligning British system stages with Montessori principles for adolescents.

## Support Services and Student Life

Imagine Montessori School Valencia provides various support and services to its community:

*   **[[Psycho Pedagogical Guidance]]**: Identifies special educational needs, advises families, and coordinates support with external specialists.
*   **[[Career Guidance]]**: Helps secondary students with vocational counselling, information on career options, and development of employability skills.
*   **[[School Nutrition Policy]]**: Offers nutritionally balanced meals made daily with organic, seasonal, and local ingredients, encouraging student participation in gardening and composting.
*   **[[School Enrichment Programme]]**: An extracurricular activities program designed to foster creativity and social skills.
*   **Communication Platforms**: Utilizes [[Phidias communication platform]] for administrative and academic communication and [[Transparent Classroom]] for pedagogical updates in Early Years and Primary.

## Operational Guidelines

The school has clear guidelines for daily operations, including [[Punctuality guidelines]] for arrival and departure, policies for [[Early Bird and Afternoon Club]], [[School Dress Code]], and [[School Health Standards]]. It emphasizes open [[Communication Between Families and the School]] and hosts [[Pedagogical Meetings]] to involve families in the educational process. A comprehensive [[School Payment Policy]] and information on [[School Financial Aid]] are also provided to families.
