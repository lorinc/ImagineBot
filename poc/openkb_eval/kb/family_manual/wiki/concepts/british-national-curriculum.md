# British National Curriculum

The British National Curriculum of England and Wales is a comprehensive educational framework that sets out the subjects and learning standards for children in state-funded schools across England. It is designed to provide a broad and balanced education, fostering key skills and knowledge in students.

At [[Imagine Montessori School Valencia]], the British National Curriculum is seamlessly integrated with the [[Montessori curriculum]]. The school is recognized and authorized by both the UK Department for Education (DfES) and the Spanish Ministry of Education, ensuring adherence to international educational standards while operating within Spain.

## Key Aspects at Imagine Montessori School Valencia

### Integration with Montessori Pedagogy
Imagine Montessori School Valencia adopts a creative and hands-on approach, combining the structured objectives of the British curriculum with the child-centered philosophy of Maria Montessori. Both approaches share common goals, such as reinforcing natural curiosity, fostering a love for learning, and developing inquisitive thinkers. This unique blend ensures that curricular goals, concepts, and skills are taught and emphasized effectively through practical, engaging activities.

### Curriculum Stages
The British National Curriculum is structured into several key stages, which are referenced in the school's operations and reporting:

*   **[[Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS)]]**: This stage covers children from ages 2 to 5 (Pre-Nursery, Nursery, Reception, and Year 1 equivalent). The primary goal is holistic development, focusing on core areas like Personal, Social, and Emotional Development (PSED), Physical Development (PD), and Communication and Language (C&L). Once foundational confidence is built, specific areas such as Literacy, Mathematics, Understanding the World, and Expressive Arts and Design are introduced. At Imagine, EYFS learning areas are addressed within the [[Montessori Work Cycle]].

*   **Key Stage 1 (KS1)**: Typically for Years 1 and 2 (ages 5-7).

*   **Key Stage 2 (KS2)**: For Primary students from Years 3 to 6 (ages 7-11). Students in Primary at Imagine are assessed using National Curriculum standards. Traditional subjects like science, history, geography, mathematics, and language and literature are covered, often within the framework of the five "[[Great Lessons (Montessori)]]". Additionally, [[STEM education]] content is enhanced through a cross-curricular "Creative Lab" workshop.

*   **Key Stage 3 (KS3)**: For Secondary students from Years 7 to 9 (ages 11-14).

*   **Key Stage 4 (KS4)**: For Secondary students from Years 10 to 11 (ages 14-16), including iGCSE qualifications.

*   **Key Stage 5 (KS5)**: For Secondary students from Years 12 to 13 (ages 16-18), including A-Levels.

### Language of Instruction
As a British school, the primary language of instruction at Imagine Montessori School Valencia is English. Families are encouraged to support their child's English language development at home as part of their commitment to this educational choice.
