# Safety Management

Safety Management refers to the comprehensive and systematic approach adopted by Imagine Montessori School to identify, evaluate, and control potential risks, thereby ensuring a safe and secure environment for all children, staff, visitors, and contractors. It integrates various policies and protocols, demonstrating a proactive commitment to well-being across diverse operational areas.

### Core Principles and Components

The school's approach to safety management is multifaceted, as detailed across several key policies in [[summaries/policy3_hard]]:

1.  **Risk Assessment and Planning**: A foundational element, [[risk assessment]]s are conducted annually or when significant changes occur. These assessments are crucial for identifying hazards, evaluating risks, and implementing appropriate control measures. This process informs the development of comprehensive emergency plans, which are validated by the Leadership Team and practiced by all staff.

2.  **Emergency Preparedness and Response**: The school implements rigorous procedures for various emergencies:
    *   **Fire and Evacuation**: Regular emergency drills (termly), clear [[emergency evacuation procedures]] for all individuals on premises, designated fire assembly points, and strict maintenance of unobstructed evacuation routes. Fire alarms and firefighting equipment undergo regular testing by certified agents.
    *   **Weather Alerts**: A structured protocol responds to weather alerts (Green, Yellow, Orange, Red) from the Generalitat Valenciana (GVA) and AEMET. Measures range from suspending outdoor activities to potential school closure and coordinated evacuation or confinement, ensuring continuous communication with families and authorities.
    *   **First Aid and Medical Emergencies**: All staff are qualified [[first aid]]ers, exceeding required ratios. Protocols cover minor accidents, serious injuries (including RIDDOR reporting), administration of medication, and hygiene for bodily fluid spillages. Specific detailed protocols address incidents like wasp stings, including [[anaphylaxis]] recognition and [[EpiPen]] administration.

3.  **Roles and Responsibilities**: [[Safety]] is a shared responsibility, with clearly defined roles:
    *   **Health and Safety Officers** and an **External Technician in Emergency Risk Assessment** identify hazards, assess risks, and implement control measures.
    *   The **Imagine Leadership Team** holds ultimate responsibility, delegating specific tasks to individuals like the HR Responsible, Facility Manager, Head Teachers, and Head of Administration.
    *   **Employees** are responsible for upholding safety procedures, participating in training, and reporting concerns.

4.  **Training and Awareness**: Continuous training is vital. All staff receive induction training on [[health and safety]] procedures, including [[risk assessment]] competence, emergency evacuation, and [[first aid]]. Awareness campaigns cover signs of illness and safe equipment use. Specific training is provided for recurrent contractors.

5.  **Facility and Equipment Safety**: Regular inspections and maintenance are critical:
    *   Daily checks of the building (indoors and outdoors), entrances, exits, fences, gates, and locks.
    *   Verification of climbing equipment and garden areas.
    *   Annual testing of electrical and gas equipment, with service histories recorded.
    *   Periodic review of generator sets and cleaning of pipes, gutters, and drains for weather emergencies.

6.  **Specialized Safety Areas**:
    *   **Food Safety**: The [[food policy]] ensures nutritional balance, hygiene, and accommodation for special diets, contributing to student well-being.
    *   **Data Security**: The [[data protection]] policy, adhering to [[GDPR]] and LOPDGDD, safeguards personal data through principles of legality, minimization, accuracy, and robust [[data security]] measures. Extensive [[risk assessment]]s are conducted for all data processing.
    *   **Compliance and Ethical Conduct**: The [[whistleblowing channel]] policy provides a confidential mechanism for reporting breaches related to [[corruption]], [[Anti-Money Laundering (AML)]], [[privacy]] breaches, [[cybersecurity]] risks, [[equal opportunities and non-discrimination]], [[sexual harassment]], [[workplace harassment]], and other non-compliance issues. This ensures that safety and ethical standards are upheld and deviations are addressed.

7.  **Monitoring and Review**: All safety-related policies are monitored and reviewed biennially by the Imagine Leadership Team, ensuring continuous improvement and adherence to current regulations and best practices.

Through these integrated policies and procedures, Imagine Montessori School maintains a robust safety management system designed to protect its community effectively.
