# Employee Training

Employee training at Imagine Montessori School is a cornerstone of its commitment to safety, health, and operational excellence. It encompasses a range of programs designed to equip staff with the necessary knowledge and skills to protect children, themselves, and effectively manage various situations, as detailed in [[summaries/policy3_hard]].

## Key Areas of Training

### 1. Emergency Preparedness and Fire Safety
Staff undergo regular training and participate in drills to ensure swift and organized responses during emergencies. This includes:
*   **Evacuation Procedures:** All new staff are trained in the correct procedures for evacuating the building in the event of a fire or other emergency.
*   **Fire Safety Training:** Recurrent contractors, such as cleaning and kitchen staff, receive basic fire safety training tailored to their roles, ensuring preparedness even outside regular school hours. This covers actions upon hearing an alarm, means of escape, assembly points, and the location of firefighting equipment.

### 2. Health and Safety Procedures
Comprehensive [[health and safety]] training is provided to ensure all staff can identify and mitigate risks, contributing to a safe environment:
*   **Induction Training:** New staff receive clear explanations of the school's Health and Safety procedures, including the hygiene regime, as part of their induction.
*   **Risk Assessment Competence:** As part of a rolling program, staff are trained in Health and Safety, including competence in [[risk assessment]], to proactively identify and manage potential hazards.
*   **Illness Awareness:** Staff are kept aware of signs of serious illnesses like meningitis, hepatitis B, and AIDS.
*   **Specific Role Training:** Maintenance staff are trained on the correct and safe use of electrical equipment and the location of main utility shut-offs (water and electricity).
*   **Ongoing Updates:** Regular staff meetings serve as forums to focus on different health and safety issues, such as staying up-to-date on tetanus vaccinations.

### 3. First Aid Training
Imagine Montessori School places a high emphasis on immediate medical response capabilities:
*   **Qualified First Aiders:** All staff are trained to be qualified [[first aid]]ers, exceeding required ratios, and undergo annual recurrent training.
*   **Emergency Protocols:** This includes specific training for critical situations, such as the proper use of an [[EpiPen]] in cases of [[anaphylaxis]] from severe allergic reactions like wasp stings.

### 4. Contractor Safety Procedures
Contractors are also integrated into the school's safety framework through tailored training:
*   **Recurrent Contractors:** Receive adapted Fire and Emergency Protocols and basic fire safety training.
*   **Non-Recurrent Contractors:** Receive a brief summary of emergency procedures upon arrival, including exit routes and assembly points, and are instructed to follow the nearest evacuation team's guidance.

By ensuring thorough and ongoing employee training, Imagine Montessori School aims to maintain a high standard of safety, preparedness, and response capability across all aspects of its operations.
