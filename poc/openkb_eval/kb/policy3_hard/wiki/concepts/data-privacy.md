# concepts/data-privacy

[[Data privacy]] refers to the protection of personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, ensuring individuals have control over their own data. It encompasses the principles, policies, and regulations that govern how personal data is collected, processed, stored, and shared.

At Imagine Montessori School, operated by ZUBI EDUCATION, S.L., [[data privacy]] is a core commitment, aligning with stringent legal frameworks such as the [[GDPR]] (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and Spain's LOPDGDD (Ley Orgánica 3/2018). The school's approach, detailed in its Data Protection Privacy Policy ([[summaries/policy3_hard]] - Data Protection Privacy Policy), emphasizes secure and transparent handling of all [[personal data]].

## Key Principles of Data Privacy
The school adheres to the following principles for managing personal data:
*   **Legality:** Data is collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes, primarily based on explicit [[consent]] or contractual necessity.
*   **Data Minimisation:** Only strictly relevant and necessary data is collected for the stated purposes.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Data is used solely for the purposes for which it was collected, and in accordance with the individual's wishes.
*   **Accuracy:** [[Personal data]] is kept accurate and up-to-date.
*   **[[Data Security]]:** Robust technical and organizational measures are implemented to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, accidental loss, destruction, or alteration of data.
*   **Retention:** Data is retained only for as long as necessary to comply with legal requirements or operational needs.
*   **International Transfers:** Transfers of data outside the EU/EEA are only conducted when adequate protection is ensured, such as through standard contractual clauses.
*   **Third Parties:** Access to and transfer of data to third parties occur in compliance with legal and regulatory obligations and with appropriate contractual safeguards.

## Legal Framework and Consent
The primary legal bases for data processing are the express [[consent]] of the individual and the necessity for performing a contract. Consent is always explicit, requiring a positive act (e.g., ticking a checkbox, signing a form) and never inferred from silence or inaction. The school maintains records of all consents given.

## Data Controller and Processed Data
ZUBI EDUCATION, S.L. is the Data Controller, with support from its parent company, [[Zubi Group Impact]], S.L. The school collects various categories of [[personal data]], including identifying information, personal characteristics, social circumstances, employment status, and banking/financial data. Sensitive data (e.g., race, political opinions, religious beliefs, health information) is generally not collected unless legally required.

## Purposes and Sharing of Personal Data
[[Personal data]] is collected to provide information, services, products, and relevant news. This data may be shared with:
*   Service providers acting on the school's behalf.
*   Independent agents, financial partners, and business partners.
*   Financial and judicial authorities, state agencies, and public bodies (when legally required or requested).
*   Regulated professionals (e.g., lawyers, auditors).
*   The parent company, [[Zubi Group Impact]], S.L.

## User Rights
Individuals (data subjects) have several rights regarding their [[personal data]], which can be exercised by contacting the school's Data Protection Officer. These rights include:
*   **Access:** Obtaining information about data processing and a copy of their data.
*   **Rectification:** Correcting inaccurate or incomplete data.
*   **Erasure:** Requesting the deletion of their data, within legal limits.
*   **Restriction:** Requesting limitations on data processing.
*   **Opposition:** Objecting to data processing based on their particular situation, especially for direct marketing purposes.
*   **Portability:** Receiving their data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format or having it transferred to another controller.
*   **Withdrawal of Consent:** Revoking previously given consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

## Integration with Other Policies
[[Data privacy]] principles are also central to other school policies, such as the [[whistleblowing channel]] policy, which ensures the confidentiality and secure handling of data related to informants and reported incidents. The school performs exhaustive [[risk assessment]]s to ensure comprehensive protection of fundamental rights.
